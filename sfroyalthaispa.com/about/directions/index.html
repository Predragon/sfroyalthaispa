    There is metered parking in front good for 4 hours. Or up the street, there is a residential area where you can park for 2 hours free. NOTICE: Do not park on Bay St. after 4 p.m. M-F as it is a tow-away zone.

    DIRECTIONS FROM BAY BRIDGE:

    1. Take Folsom St. exit Go for 0.5 mi/.
    2. Turn left onto Folsom St. Go for 0.3 mi/.
    3. Turn left onto The Embarcadero. Go for 1.5 mi/.
    4. Bear left onto North Point St. Go for 0.6 mi/.
    5. Your destination on North Point St is on the left.
